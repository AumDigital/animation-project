Our Gardens...

Cacti are a type of succulent plant that is known for its spiny appearance and ability to store water in its thick, fleshy stem. Cacti are native to the Americas, where they can be found in a range of habitats from arid deserts to tropical rainforests.

They come in a variety of shapes and sizes, from the small and round to the towering and columnar. Cacti have adapted to survive in harsh and dry environments by developing specialized features such as thorns to deter animals from eating them and the ability to close their stomata (pores on their surface) during the day to reduce water loss.
